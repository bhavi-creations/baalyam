"Step into Lower Kindergarten at Baalyam Preschool, where learning is an adventure filled with exploration and discovery. Through our unique approach of teaching via touch and feel, children immerse themselves in a world of sensory learning, grasping concepts with ease.

With no homework or notebooks, our focus is on nurturing a love for languages and instilling a lifelong passion for learning. We prioritize the health and safety of our students with regular health checkups, vaccinations, and a hygienic environment.

Additionally, our curriculum includes teachings of mythology and quality discussions, fostering holistic development.

With security measures in place at every step, parents can rest assured knowing their child is in good hands. Our vibrant programs, including festival day celebrations and color day festivities, add joy and excitement to the learning journey, making every day a new and memorable experience for our young learners."
