n Upper Kindergarten at Baalyam Preschool, learning takes on an adventurous twist as children explore different classrooms for varied learning experiences.

With no homework or notebooks, our focus remains on interactive teaching methods. We prioritize the health and safety of our students with frequent health checkups, vaccines, and a hygienic environment. Our curriculum extends beyond academics, incorporating mythology to instill ethics and morals.

Additionally, we offer foreign language classes to enhance linguistic skills and understanding. Quality talks and ample security measures ensure a conducive environment for holistic development. Exciting programs like festival and color day celebrations add to the joyous learning experience.
