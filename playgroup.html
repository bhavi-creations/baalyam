Play Group

Welcome to our vibrant Playgroup! Designed for children aged 2 years and above, our nurturing environment blends home comforts with exciting early education. With engaging activities, language enrichment, and fun learning experiences, we ensure every child feels at home while exploring the world.

Parent involvement is key, making education a shared journey. Join us and nurture the joy of learning from the very start!

At Baalyam Nursery's Playgroup, we believe in fostering a love for learning from the very beginning. Our curriculum is designed to spark curiosity and encourage exploration, laying the foundation for a lifetime of discovery. With a focus on social-emotional development and early literacy skills, we provide a supportive environment where children can grow and thrive.

Our play-based approach allows children to learn through play, promoting creativity, problem-solving, and social skills development.

With no homework or notebooks, our focus remains on interactive teaching methods. We prioritize the health and safety of our students with frequent health checkups, vaccines, and a hygienic environment. Our curriculum extends beyond academics, incorporating mythology to instill ethics and morals.
